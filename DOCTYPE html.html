Academic Success

Skills, resources, and strategies for your learning at MSU.

  • Manage your time using calendars, planners, and weekly reviews.
  • Engage in active study: Pomodoro sessions, practice tests, and group study.
  • Attend office hours consistently to clarify concepts and build relationships with instructors.
  • Take organized notes using methods like Cornell Notes or digital tools like Notion.

Resources:

  • MSU Writing Center
  • MSU Study Groups
  • Notion

Community Engagement

Building connections and belonging at MSU.

  • Join student organizations to expand your social network.
  • Participate in volunteer opportunities and service-learning projects.
  • Seek mentorship from faculty or upperclassmen for guidance.
  • Attend campus events to strengthen your sense of belonging.

Resources:

  • Experience MSU
  • MSU Service Learning
  • MSU Mentorship Programs

Health & Wellness

Fitness, mental health, and financial literacy for success.

  • Maintain physical health with regular workouts and recreational sports.
  • Manage stress and mental health through counseling, meditation, or journaling.
  • Track finances and develop budgeting skills for financial wellness.
  • Get adequate sleep and nutrition to support academic and personal performance.

Resources:

  • MSU Rec Sports
  • Counseling & Psychiatric Services (CAPS)
  • MSU Financial Literacy
